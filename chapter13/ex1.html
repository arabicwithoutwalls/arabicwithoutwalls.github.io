What are the Pan-Arab Colors?

Four of the colors in the chart below figure most frequently in modern Arab flags. They are known as the Pan-Arab flag colors, referring to the four colors adopted for the banner of the Arab Revolt of 1916. Examine the flags carefully before going over this chart. Then proceed to check the Yes box if the color is one of these four Pan-Arab colors and check No if it is not.

Symbols الرموز in the Arab Flags
