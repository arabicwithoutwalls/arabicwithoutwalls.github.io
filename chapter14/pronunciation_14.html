Pronunciation Tip

In Chapter 1 you learned several question words and different combinations of them such as مِن أين؟ and مع مَن؟. There are other possible combinations of question words with prepositions such as:

عن ماﺫا؟
عن مَن؟
مِن مَن؟
بماﺫا؟
لِماﺫا؟
مِن ماﺫا؟

These question words have a contracted form for easier pronunciation.  Listen to the contracted forms given in the table below and practice saying them aloud.

عَمﱠ؟ عن ماﺫا؟
عَمَّن؟ عن مَن؟
مِمﱠ؟ مِن ماﺫا؟
مِمَّن؟ مِن مَن؟
بِمَ؟ بماﺫا؟ 
لِمَ؟ لِماﺫا؟

Practice
Listen to these examples and note how the question words are pronounced.

۱- عمﱠ تكتب؟  Meaning
What are you writing about?

۲- لِم لا تسكن مع أسرتك؟  Meaning
Why don’t you live with your family?

٣- عمّن تتكلّم؟  Meaning
Who are you talking about?

٤- بـِم تشعر؟  Meaning
How (What) are you feeling?

Speak to Your Instructor
Practice listening and saying the following sentences in preparation for the chat session where you will be asked to read them aloud to your instructor.

۱- عمّ يتكلـّم هذا الكتاب؟  Meaning
What is this book talking about?

۲- بم شعرت؟  Meaning
How (what) did she feel?

٣- لم لا تلتحق بالجامعة؟  Meaning
Why don’t you (masculine singular) go to college?

٤- ممّ يتكوّن هذا الصفّ؟  Meaning
What does this course consist of?
