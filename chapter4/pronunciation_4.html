Negation of الفعل المضارع with لا

In Chapter 4, you have learned الفعل المضارع and how to negate it with the particle لا. The intonation of affirmative sentences in the present tense differs from that of sentences in which the verb is negated. When the negation is introduced, the negation particleلا bears the stress.

Practice
Listen carefully to the following examples and try to discern the difference in the intonation of the verb in affirmative and negative sentences.  Practice saying these sentences aloud making sure to stress لا.

۱- أنا أذهب إلى الجامعة كلّ يوم. Meaning

I go to school everyday.

أنا لا أذهب إلى الجامعة كلّ يوم. Meaning
I do not go to school everyday.

۲- هي تسافر كلّ سنة. Meaning

She travels every year.

هي لا تسافركلّ سنة. Meaning
She does not travel every year.

۳- أنا أتذكّر أصدقاء طفولتي. Meaning

I remember my childhood friends.

أنا لا أتذكّر أصدقاء طفولتي. Meaning
I do not remember my childhood friends.

Speak to Your Instructor
Practice listening and saying the following sentences in preparation for the chat session where you will be asked to read them aloud to your instructor.

۱- أقاربي لا يعيشون في أمريكا. Meaning

My relatives do not live in the States.

۲- أنا لا أعرف سعاد زوجة خالي مسعود. Meaning
I do not know Souad, my uncle Massoud’s wife.

۳- والدي يدرّس في الجامعة. Meaning
My father teaches at the university.

٤- أنا لا أعرف خالاتي جيّداً. Meaning
I do not know my aunts (maternal) very well.

٥- أنا أعرف خالاتي جيّداً. Meaning
I know my aunts (maternal) very well.
