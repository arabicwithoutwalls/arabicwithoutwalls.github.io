Drill 11

Practice using القاموس by looking up the following words. First, identify الجذر. After you write down what you think it is, click on the الجذر button next to the word to check your answer. Then look up the word in القاموس (we recommend the standard "Hans Wehr," which you can see and read about at: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dictionary_of_Modern_Written_Arabic). If الجذر is familiar to you, then try and guess what the meaning of the new word might be, then look it up and write down what you find. For some entries you'll probably find a lot of options so be sure to check your answer by clicking the answer button to see one of the most common meanings of the word.
