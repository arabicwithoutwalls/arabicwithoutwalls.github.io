Interview

الأستاذ علي

In this video علي tells you about his occupation. If you can answer any portion of the question in Arabic, please do. Otherwise, answer the questions in English.

Vocabulary
stage, level      مرحلة

 

I. Read questions a and b. After you read them, watch the video and listen for the answer.


He is a teacher.a. In general, what does علي do?  Answer


في مدارس _____b. Where does he work?  Hint

II. Now it is time to practice guessing new vocabulary from context.


You might notice that the first word, درست, sounds like study or class.Notice that the first word علي says in this video. Have you heard anything that sounds similar?
  Answer


درست : I studied (past tense)Use the context to guess its meaning. You know that علي tells you he teaches something. The first part of the video goes something like this (with blanks on unfamiliar words):
_______ Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, and now I am a teacher of those _______ in the French schools. What makes sense as the first word (before Physics)?  Answer

Conclusion: In this lesson you had the opportunity to practice listening strategies. Remember that as you listen to speakers in Arabic, you should focus on vocabulary you know to learn the basic meaning of what they say and intelligently guess the meaning of unfamiliar words.
