Listening (Dialogue C)

Listen to the following dialogue and then answer the questions based on it. There are two sets of questions. Answer and check the first set before moving on to the second.

Note: Remember هذا and لي ? You learned these two words for previous listening exercises. You also need to know the vocabulary from Alif Baa, namely بناية . Do you need a refresher?

Background: Widad, a female college student at Beir Zeit University, stops another student on campus.

First Set of Questions

  1. Why does Widad strike up a conversation with the other student? hint To help you focus in on the answer, listen to the pertinent excerpt from the conversation.
    to ask the time (so that she is not late for class)
    to ask for directions to class
    to find out who teaches her class
    to find out what her new class is like
