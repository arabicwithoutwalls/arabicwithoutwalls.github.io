Grammar Note 1

The Superlative - أفْعَل التفضيل

To read in conjunction with AK pages 209-210.

أفْعَل التفضيل is formed by stripping the adjective down to its root جذر and inserting it into the pattern: أفْعَل that is, adding أ at the beginning followed by a ــْ (سكون) after the first consonant of the root, and a ــَ (fatHa) between the second and third consonants.

Example:

كبيرك-ب-رأكْبَر
باردب-ر-دأبْرَد
مشهورش-هـ-رأشْهَر

The superlative is formed slightly differently when:
1- The root ends in a vowel, e.g.,    عالي -  ع ل ي
2- The root has a double consonant, e.g.,     حارّ - ح رر

Each of these subcategories creates a consistent pattern.

Ends in a vowelDouble consonant
عالي – أعْلى حارّ – أحَرّ
غالي – أغلى جديد – أجَدّ
ذكيّ – أذكى قليل – أقَلّ
