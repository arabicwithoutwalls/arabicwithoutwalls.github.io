Video Activity

ديانة

In this activity ديانة will talk about her career goals. In earlier lessons, you have had the opportunity to hear different individuals talk about what they do, where they work, or what they study. Now, you will hear someone talk not only about what she does, but about her aspirations as well. Follow the instructions carefully to learn about this speaker's goals.

 

I. Before watching the video, brainstorm for a minute about the way you ask someone about his or her future career or educational plans. What questions would you ask? Next, watch the video once all the way through.

Conclusion: In this activity, you learned how to talk about some of your future goals and how to ask others about theirs. To practice what you have learned, get with a partner and have a short conversation about your current studies and future goals. Use the questions that you heard the interviewer use to ask your partner what he or she wants to do, and use the new vocabulary that you heard to answer the same questions.
