Complete the following sentences that answer "لِماذا" by choosing the appropriate word from the drop-down menu. Remember that when لـِ is added on to الـ the combination is spelled لِلـْ. Also remember that لأنّ attaches itself to the pronoun suffix that follows it to form one word as in لأنّ followed by ه making لأنّه.
