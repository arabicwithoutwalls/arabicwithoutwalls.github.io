بيت للإيجار

Imagine that you are managing a rental company. A potential tenant has approached you and is asking you about one of the houses your company is renting out. Answer his/her questions. Record your answers and send them as a sound file to your instructor. Click on sample answers for ideas on how to answer the questions if necessary.
