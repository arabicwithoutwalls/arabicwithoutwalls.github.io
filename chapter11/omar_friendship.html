عمر: الأصدقاء

In this activity, عمر tells us more about himself. Specifically, he talks about the basis for different friendships that he has. Before you watch the video, think about your own friendships. What types of interest or activities do you share with your friends? Write some of them down so that you can be thinking about them as you watch the video.

Now, watch the video for the first time. You’ll hear a phrase repeated several times. The context in which you hear it the first time is as follows: الهواية ____________. Listen to the video again all the way through, and try to pick out the phrase.


عن طريق1. Write what you think the phrase is.  Answer


عن طريق = through, by way of, or more context specific: based on.
Try using the context to guess the meaning of this phrase. First of all, we already know that he is talking about friendships and the basis for different friendships that he has. Second, he says things like لنا صداقات عن طريق... followed by specific things.2. What does عن طريق mean?  Answer | Hint
