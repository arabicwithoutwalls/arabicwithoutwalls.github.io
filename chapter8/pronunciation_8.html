Pronunciation Tip

Past Tense Negation with ما

In this chapter, you learned the past tense الفعل الماضي and its negation with the particle ما. When negation is introduced, the negation particle ما becomes stressed.

Example:

۱- والدي رفض مساعدتي.  Meaning

My father refused to help me.

۲- والدي ما رفض مساعدتي.  Meaning

My father did not refuse to help me.

Practice
Listen carefully to the following examples and try to discern the difference in the intonation of the verb in affirmative and negative sentences.  Practice putting the stress on ما.

۱-
أ. أنا ذهبت إلى لوس أنجلس الأسبوع الماضي.  Meaning

I went to Los Angeles last week.

ب. أنا ما ذهبت إلى لوس أنجلس الأسبوع الماضي.  Meaning

I did not go to Los Angeles last week.

۲-
أ. هو قرأ كلّ الكتاب.  Meaning

He read the entire book.

ب. هو ما قرأ كلّ الكتاب.  Meaning

He did not read the entire book.

Speak to Your Instructor
Practice listening and saying the following sentences in preparation for the chat session where you will be asked to read them aloud to your instructor.

۱- أنا ما نجحت في صفّ الكيمياء.  Meaning

I did not pass the chemistry class.

۲- أنا ما حصلت على شهادة الدكتوراه.  Meaning

I did not get a Ph.D.

۳- تخرّجتْ سُهى من الجامعة اللبنانيّة السنة الماضية.  Meaning

Soha graduated from the Lebanese University last year.

٤- كتب لي عمّي رسالة .  Meaning

My uncle wrote me a letter.

٥- ما شاهدت التلفزيون ليلة أمس.  Meaning

I did not watch television last night.
