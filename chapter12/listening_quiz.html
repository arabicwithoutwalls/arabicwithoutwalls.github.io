Listening Quiz

As you try to answer the following questions, you may watch the accompanying video only three times.

Vocabulary
to leaveترك
increaseازداد
attachment (to someone)تعلق

 

1. Where is the girl in this video from? How do you know?  

2. She moved from Algeria and ended up where?  

3. What does Muhammad specifically want to know about her move from Algeria?  

4. How did she feel about leaving Algeria?  

5. She mentions how her relationship changed with one member of her family. Which member of the family does she mention, and what was the nature of the change?
