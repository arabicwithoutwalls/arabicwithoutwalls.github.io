Video Activity

يا عمي، يا عمتي

Prepare to listen: Reveiw family realtionship words, such as brother, uncle, etc. Be sure to review both singular and plural forms. Then learn the glossed vocabulary, which follows.

Vocabulary:
أربعين : forty
تمنين : eighty (colloquial)
شقيق : brother / sibling
قرية : town / village

Introduction: In this challenging exercise, عمر talks about his extended family. Make sure you have memorized the new vocabulary in the glossary above before watching the video.

 

Listen carefully to the video once or twice, keeping the following questions in mind:



He mentions that there are 3 brothers and 2 sisters.

a. How many brothers and sisters does عمر say are in his family?

   Answer

more than 40

b. عمر mentions a very large number of uncles. How many does he say he has?

   Answer

He mentions that he called 80 people عمتي.

c. How many people does he call aunt?

   Answer


لم يقل... : Did not say / Did not call...

Watch the video again. This time, listen for more detail. Specifically, think about the large number of aunts and uncles that عمر has mentioned. Do you think that all of them were his parents; brothers and sisters? He answers this question. Listen for the answer.

   Hint
 

Conclusion: After this lesson, you should have an idea about how close-knit Arabic communities can be. While many of the people that عمر calls عمي or عمتي are not literally his aunts and uncles, they are close enough in the community to be considered like family.
