Personalized Practice

خالتي مترجمة جيّدة

In a casual conversation with an acquaintance you found yourself recommending your maternal aunt for a translation position at their company. Answer the following questions as fully and as positively as possible to help your aunt get the job. Use "Voice Boards" in Wimba to record your answers and save them for your instructor. Click on sample answers for ideas on how to answer the questions if necessary. Technical requirements
