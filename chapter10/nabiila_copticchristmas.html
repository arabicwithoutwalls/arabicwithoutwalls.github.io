نبيلة: عيد الميلاد في مصر

This is a challenging video that will give you some insights into Egyptian culture. Specifically, you will learn a little about the Copts. Copts make up the majority of Christians in Egypt, and they have deep historic roots in that country. As you work through these questions, do not worry about trying to understand everything that is said. Instead, try to get the information sufficient to answer the questions.
