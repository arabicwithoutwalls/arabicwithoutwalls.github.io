Pronunciation Tip

Dagger Alif

In Arabic, there are a few words where the short vowel ــَ is pronounced as the long vowel ا. This type of unwritten but pronounced ا is called dagger alif. The following are a few words that contain dagger alif. Click to hear them pronounced.

1- The dagger alif is after the second الله :لـ Meaning

God

2- The dagger alif is after the هَذِه :هـ Meaning

This (feminine singular)

3- The dagger alif is after the هَذا :هـ Meaning

This (masculine singular)

4- The dagger alif is after the ذَلِكَ :ذ Meaning

That (masculine singular)

5- The dagger alif is after the لِذَلِكَ :ذ Meaning

That is why

6- The dagger alif is after the كـَذَلِكَ :ذ Meaning
