Listening - Dialogue C

Listen to the dialogue and then answer the questions below.

Remember not to print out any dialogues until after you have listened to them and completed the questions based on them.

  1. How does Mona reply to her friend's saying goodbye?
    Allaah yisallimak الله يسلّمَك
    Allaah yisallimik الله يسلّمِك
  2. Why does she reply in this manner?
    Because Mona is a male.
    Because Mona is a female.
    Because Mona's friend (Samir) is male.
    Because Mona's friend (Samira) is female.

Explanation

  1. Mona is speaking to another female. Allaah yisallimik الله يسلّمِك is the female form of the response.
  2. Mona's gender is irrelevant. In the case of Allaah yisallimik الله يسلّمِك, the greeting matches the gender of the person who is intended to receive it, and not the person who gives it.
