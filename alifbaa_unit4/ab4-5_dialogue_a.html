Listening (Dialogue A)

Listen to the following dialogue and then answer the questions based on it.

  1. Which speaker introduces himself first?
  2. Out of the following selections, which is the most likely place of origin for Galib?
  3. What doesn't Galib know?


The four al-iDaafa الإضافة used in this dialogue follow in the order they first appeared.

مدينة دمشق
mediinat dimashq
the city of Damascus

مدينة شكاغو
mediinat shikaagu
the city of Chicago

ولاية واشنطون
wilaayat washinTuun
the state of Washington

ولاية إلنوي
wilaayat ilinuwi
