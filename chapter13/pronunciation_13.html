Pronuncation Tip

ألف المدّة in Verbs Starting with أ

When conjugated in المضارع with the first person singular, verbs whose root begins with a أ end up with two consecutive أ at the beginning, one followed by a  ــَـ  and the second followed by a  ــْـ. This necessitates an alternative spelling and pronunciation where the two أ are contracted to form an آ that is pronounced as أ followed by an ا.

Example:

أ + أكل = آكل
أنا آكل تفّاحة.  

Practice
Practice listening and repeating the following sentences and notice how the أ أ combination turned into آ.

۱- أنا آخذ كلّ الصور.  MeaningI take all the pictures.
۲- أنا أحبّ أن آكل الباستا كثيرًا.  MeaningI like eating pasta a lot.
