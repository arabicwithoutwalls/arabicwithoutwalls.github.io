HOW TO USE لكن AND لأن

Using لكن and لأن with Pronouns

If the subject of the clause لكن or لأن is a pronoun, it transforms into a pronoun suffix. This means that the pronoun changes form and is attached to the end of the relevant word. For example, لكن + لأن is expressed لكنّي.

At this point, we do not expect you to learn all the pronoun suffixes. Just be aware of how "I," "he," and "she" are used in conjunction with لكن and لأن.

لكنّي 
laakinni but I 		لأنّي 
li'anni because I
لكنّه 
laakinnahu but he 		لأنّه 
li'aanahu becaue he
لكنّها 
laakinnaha but she 		لأنّها 
li'aanaha becaue she
