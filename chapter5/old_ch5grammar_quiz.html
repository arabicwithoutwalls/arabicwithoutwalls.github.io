Grammar Quiz

This quiz contains a number of items that deal with the definite article ال. Please be sure to review how the definite article is used in different constructions, including those in the following list.

  • to show definiteness and indefiniteness (Alif Baa, Chapter 8; Al-Kitaab, Chapter 2)
  • noun-adjective agreement (Al-Kitaab, Chapter 1)
  • in iDaafas (Al-Kitaab, Chapter 3)
  • in noun adjective phrases (Al-Kitaab, Chapter 5)
  • with هذا \ هذه (Al-Kitaab, Chapter 5)
  • with special vocabulary that require—or don't require—the definite article
