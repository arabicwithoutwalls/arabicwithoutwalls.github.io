Chat Activity

Meet a Classmate

Step 1
Before logging on to introduce yourself to your virtual classmates, practice using the expressions that you encountered so far by responding aloud to the following prompts.  In Exercise 1, you will respond, but in Exercise 2, you will initiate the conversation.  Click to hear a model response when necessary.

Exercise 1
Respond to the following greetings and questions.

أهلا وسهلا. Example

اِسمي سالم، واِسم حضرتك؟ Example

Example تشرّفنا، حضرتك من أمريكا؟

Exercise 2
Formulate the expressions and questions that initiate the following answers.

Exampleأهلا بيك.

اِسمي علي. Example
Example أيوه، أنا من القاهرة.

Step 2
Now log on to meet a classmate, using as many of the Arabic expressions in Units 1 and 2 as possible.  See the "Communication Tools" section of the Technical Requirements page for information on how to set up a chat.
