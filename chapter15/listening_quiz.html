Listening Quiz

Without using any books or materials, answer the following questions based on the video. You may listen to the video as many times as you need.


 


1. Where does the majority of her father's family live?  


2. Her mother permits her to go out with friends but is angry if what?  


3. Why does her sister have more freedom than her?  


4. What does her mother not allow Razan to do?  


5. Does she feel that she is Palestinian? Why or why not?
