Superlative - التفضيل

You have learned two words that have superlative meaning: أحسن and أكبر. Note that these words share a pattern. The following sentences give you similar words; expand your vocabulary by using analogy to determine the meaning of these sentences.


Khalid is the oldest boy in his family.١- خالد أكبر ولد في أسرته.
