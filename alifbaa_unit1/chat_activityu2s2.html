Chat Activity - Unit 2, Scene 2

Meet a Classmate

Step 1
Before logging on to introduce yourself to your virtual classmates, practice using the expressions that you encountered so far by responding aloud to the following prompts.  In Exercise 1, you will respond but in Exercise 2, you will initiate the conversation.  Click to hear a model response when necessary. See Technical Requirements.
