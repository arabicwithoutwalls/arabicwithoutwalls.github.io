لو أصبحتُ مليونيرًا/مليونيرة

We all dream of winning a lot of money and becoming millionaires. Answer these following questions to tell us what you would do if you won the lottery and became very rich. Record your answers and send them as a sound file to your instructor. Click on the sample answers for ideas on how to answer the questions if necessary.
