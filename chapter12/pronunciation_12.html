Pronciation Tip

الذي والتي

In the relative pronouns الذي والتي we stress the لـ and pronounce it as if it had a شدّة.  Although pronounced, this شدّة is not spelled. The same applies to the masculine plural form الذين where also the شدّة is pronounced but not written.

Practice
Read the following sentences and notice how these relative pronouns are spelled and then listen to the sentences and note how they are pronounced.

۱- هذا الكتاب الذي قرأته أمس.  Meaning

This is the book I read yesterday.


۲- أنا أحبّ المدن التي فيها بنايات كبيرة.  Meaning

I like cities that have tall buildings.

Speak to Your Instructor
Practice listening and saying the following sentences in preparation for the chat session where you will be asked to read them aloud to your instructor.

۱- صديقي الذي يدرس معي في الجامعة سافر إلى الأردن.  Meaning

My friend who studies with me at the university traveled to Jordan.


۲- هي تذهب إلى السوق في الأيّام التي ليس عندها محاضرات.  Meaning

She goes to the market on the days when she does not have classes.


٣- البيت الذي تسكن فيه عائلتي جميل جدًا.  Meaning

The house that my family lives in is very nice.


٤- الفتاة التي تعرّفت عليها أمس يمنيّة.  Meaning

The young woman that I met yesterday is Yemeni.
