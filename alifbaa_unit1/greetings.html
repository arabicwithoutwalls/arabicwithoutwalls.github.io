Greetings
Most greetings in Arabic require fixed responses. Therefore, greetings are learned in pairs: the original greeting and the required reply.

Original Greetingأهلان وسهلا
'ahlan wa sahlan
Hello.
Replyأهلا بيك
'ahlan biik
Hello to you (to a male).

Please take note of this as you study the phrases and vocabulary for this lesson.
