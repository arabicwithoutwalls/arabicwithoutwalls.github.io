Instructor Notes

Comprehensive Interview (Units 6/7/8)

Read students' Comprehensive Interview instructions for units 6/7/8.

Part I:Carry on a conversation with the student about each of your residences, fields of study, likes and dislikes, etc. Be descriptive and share, as well as ask students to share. Below are some questions you can ask to keep the conversation giong. Although the students have not explicitly learned these questions, they should be familiar enough with the main vocabulary to guess what you are asking. Go slow, and encourage students to take convidence in their ability to guess.

Questions to Discuss Residence (Address and Telephone Number)
أين تسكن (أنت)؟
في أي شارع؟
كم العنوان؟
وكيف الشارع؟
وكيف البيت؟
مع من تسكن؟
كم رقم تلفونك؟

Questions to Discuss Field of Study
ماذا تدرس (أنت)؟
وكيف ____؟ هل هو صعب؟
لماذا تدرس ___؟

Questions to Discuss Likes and Dislikes
ماذا تحّب (أنت)؟
لماذا تحّب ___؟
ماذا لا تحبّ؟
لماذا لا تحّب ___؟

Part II: When you have exhausted these topics, talk to the student about his/her mother and father. To limit confusion, use the terms ماما and بابا. Let them know that they needn't answer in complete sentences (in fact, they shouldn't) and that their focus should be on listening and guessing meaning. Feel free to share information about your parents as well.

Example Quesions
ما اسم ماما\بابا؟
أين يسكن بابا وماما؟
أين يعمل بابا؟
هل تعمل ماما؟ أين؟ في البيت؟ في شركة؟
ماذا يحبّ بابا؟ \ ماذا تحبّ ماما؟
من يحبّ بابا؟ \ ماما تحبّ بابا؟

Part III: Finally, allow the students to ask you any language questions they might have.
