Grammar Quiz

Alaa Al Aswany is an Egyptian writer and dentist, famous for his book, The Yacoubian Building. Published in 2002 and later made into a film and a television series, it gives an unforgiving picture of modern Egyptian society through the lives of various tenants of the Yacoubian Building.

In addition to being an author, Al Aswany is a dentist. Writers in Egypt, even best-selling authors like Alaa Al Aswany, rarely make enough money to support themselves. Other medical professionals who have played a leading role in literary circles include the prominent feminist Nawal Al-Sa'dawi and Yousef Idris.
