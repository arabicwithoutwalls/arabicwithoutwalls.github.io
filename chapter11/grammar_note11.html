Grammar Note

Quantifiers - بعض/معظم/كل

To review in conjunction with AK page 195.

As you learn these quantifying terms, keep in mind that certain terms form an إضافة with the noun that follows, e.g., كلّ الناس, while others designating part of a group are followed by the preposition مِن, e.g., كثير مِن الناس.

... مِن إضافة
قليل مِن  بعض 
كثير مِن  معظم 
عدد مِن  كل
