Chapter 9 Guide

Use this chapter guide to navigate through the activities of this chapter and to track your progress. There is a dot to the left of every activity description. A grey dot means that the activity is unattempted, a yellow dot means the activity has been attempted but is incomplete, and a green dot means the activity has been completed.

Some activities will automatically calculate your score when completed, and some do not. The ones that do not will require you to indicate when they have been completed. These activities have a green boxed check next to the activity title. When you have completed the activity, click on this check to record your completion of that activity.
