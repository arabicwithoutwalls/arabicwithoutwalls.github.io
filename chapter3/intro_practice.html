أصلا means "originally."2. After giving his name, حبيب uses the phrase أصلي من تونس. You may not have seen this construction before, but you have seen the word أصلا in a previous video activity (Chapter 1). Do you remember what it means? أصلي has a similar meaning. Now, how would you use this new phrase to indicate where you you are originally from?  Answer | Hint


Living/residing or I am living/residing. Learn this word. It will be useful in telling where you live when you're traveling.3. When حبيب says مقيم حاليا في باريس, what does مقيم mean? Again, use the words you know as context to guess the meaning of this new word. For example, you know that the previous sentence means "I am originally from Tunisia." From the following sentence, you know: "______ at this time in Paris." Given the context, what must the blank mean?
 Answer
Good job. This was good practice in guessing the meaning of unfamiliar words. Remember to attack unfamiliar words strategically. Check the context and surrounding words for clues to their meaning. This will often help you guess at least the general meaning.
