Listening (Dialogue C)

Listen to the dialogue and then answer the questions below.

  1. What is the first speaker's name?
    Shahira
    Marra
    Samira
  2. What is the second speaker's name?
    Shahira
    Marra
    Samira
  3. The first speaker does not understand something the second speaker says. What is it?
    the second speaker's greeting
    the second speaker's name
    the second speaker's request to repeat something
  4. What are the final words of these two speakers to each other?
    Goodbye.
    (Have a) good evening.
    Nice to meet you.

Explanation

  1. Shahira is the name of the second speaker, but the first speaker gives her name before Shahira does. Listen for when the first speaker says ismi... ...اسمي. marra مرّة is not a name. It is the first word in the phrase marra thaaniyya مرّة ثانية ("again").
  2. marra مرّة is not a name. It is the first word in the phrase marra thaaniyya مرّة ثانية ("again"). Samira is the name of the first speaker, but the second speaker says her name later in the dialogue. Listen for when the first speaker says ismi... ...اسمي.
  3. The first speaker makes her request after the second speaker says, "ismi shahiira اسمي شهيرة." The first speaker is the only one who makes this request in the dialogue; the second speaker does not.
  4. We do not hear the speakers say maca ssalaama مع سلامة and allah yisallimik الله يسلّمك ("goodbye") or masaa' il-kheer مساء الخير and masaa' in-nuur مساء النور ("good evening").
