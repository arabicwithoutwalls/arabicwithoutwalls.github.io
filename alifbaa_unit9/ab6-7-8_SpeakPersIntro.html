Personal Practice

Speaking (Personal Introduction)

Use "Voice Boards" in Wimba to record a personal introduction for yourself that includes the following information:

  • name
  • origin
  • current residence, including your street (شارع) using the following pattern:
    _____ أسكن في مدينة ______ في شارع
  • occupation(s), including location
  • what you study (Refer to our list of University Majors in Arabic.)
  • what you love (Refer to our list of Pastimes and Hobbies in Arabic.)

You may use notes, but you should practice beforehand repeatedly until your speech is fluid and natural. You may not re-record your submission, so be prepared.
