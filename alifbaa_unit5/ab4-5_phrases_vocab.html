Phrases

من أين أنتَ؟ / من أين أنتِ؟
min 'ayna 'anta? (to a male)
min 'ayna 'anti? (to a female)
Where are you from?
grammatical dissection There is no "to be" verb in Arabic; hence, this question does not contain a verb. Translated word for word, it reads: "From where you?" 		أنا من...
'ana min...
I am from...
grammatical dissection There is no "to be" verb in Arabic; hence, this sentence does not contain a verb. Translated word for word it reads: "I from..."
لا أعرف.
laa 'acrif.
I don't know.
grammatical dissection laa لا means "no," and 'acrif أعرف is a conjugated verb meaning "I know."
